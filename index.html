CSV format: your file can have as many columns as you like, just make sure you have a header row which defines at least the following columns: from (3 letter IATA code), to (3 letter IATA code), date (any format, only rendered as a string at the moment). Add a flight column if you also want links to external flight/route information.

* distances calculated are great-circle distances between airports, your actual distance travelled (including delays and indirect flight paths) will on average be 9% higher.

** CO2 calculation assumes 115g per passenger km (average emission for short international flights1, 2) and does not take into account either the average 9% increase for realistic flight routes or radiative forcing (which increases your emissions by about a factor of two).
